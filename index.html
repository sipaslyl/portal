About Me

Hello! My name is Lylla Sipaseuth and I have a love for cute colorful things. After completing my Associate of Arts in Computer Science in 2019, I joined the Year Up program based on my enthusiasm for programming and software development. I have a keen interest in programming languages and learning new things. I always do my best to learn from every situation and move forward with that in mind.

I enjoy learning new programming languages and finding ways to implement the concepts. Helping others further their understanding is also an area I excel in. I am able to help others understand the code and then increase my own understanding by teaching.

My interest in video games leads me to want to be involved in the development of video games. I want to be a part of the creation of video games. Whether mobile or console or PC, I have an interest in all areas of video game programming and design.
